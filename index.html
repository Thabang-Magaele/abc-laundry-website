Our story

Your neighbourhood laundry, done right

ABC Laundry & Dry Cleaners has been keeping the community looking sharp for years. We're a family-run business built on a simple promise: treat every garment as if it were our own.

From quick wash-and-fold loads to delicate dry cleaning, our team uses modern, eco-friendly equipment and genuine care. The result? Clothes that look better, last longer and come back exactly when we promised.
